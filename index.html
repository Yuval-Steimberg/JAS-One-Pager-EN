Who We Are

Israel's First Venture
for Reuse and Upcycling

Just A Second was founded to create systemic change in the way Israel treats materials and objects. We collect items from streets, urban renewal projects, and businesses — repairing, redesigning, and giving them new life.

All profits go toward supporting the mental health of combat reserve soldiers.

What We Do

From the Street to Home

Urban Renewal

Selective dismantling of apartments in urban renewal projects. Rescuing furniture and architectural elements before demolition.

Street Treasures

Collecting discarded furniture from Tel Aviv streets. Restoring, redesigning, and giving them new life.

Malls & Industry

Sustainable clearance solutions for shops and businesses. In partnership with the Azrieli Group.

JAS Original

A leading design studio — creating new items from discarded products. Led by a product designer alongside reserve soldiers and volunteers.

Gallery Shop

Every Item Is
One of a Kind

Our gallery shop showcases all “street treasures” that have gone through a meticulous upcycling process. Furniture, lighting, housewares, art, and more — every item has a story.

No two items are alike. Each one has gone on its own journey and become a gift or piece of furniture with personal and emotional value.
